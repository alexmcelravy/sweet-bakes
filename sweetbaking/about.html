About Sweet Baking

Welcome to Sweet Baking! I have always loved baking, as a kid I would watch my mom bake for hours. When I was about 8 I got my first Easy Bake Oven and never stopped from there. I eventually transitioned to a real oven and stove, and became a self-taught baker in my free time.

Sweet Baking is meant to be a baking blog/collection of my favorite dessert recipes. The purpose of this website is to share my recipes with other bakers in an easy-to-read format, and document my learning process as a self-taught baker. Having recipes all in one place makes this website an easy place to access your favorite recipes without having to shuffle for lost recipe cards. I hope you find Sweet Baking insightful and helpful, as I continue to share my baking journey!
