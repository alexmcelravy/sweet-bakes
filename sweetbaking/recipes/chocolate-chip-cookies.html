Chocolate Chip Cookies

Soft and chewy chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup of softened butter (3 sticks)
  • 1 1/4 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 1 1/4 cup of white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla
  • 3 eggs at room temperature
  • 4 1/2 cup of flour
  • 2 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Cream butter and salt.
  2. Beat in eggs one at a time.
  3. Add vanilla.
  4. Mix dry ingredients and add slowly to butter/sugar/egg mixture
  5. Stir in chocolate chips. Refrigerate dough for 1 hour before baking for softer cookie.
  6. Bake at 375 degrees for 12-15 minutes until golden brown and soft in the middle.
